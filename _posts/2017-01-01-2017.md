<p class="caption"><strong>Virgin Mary</strong><br>2017</p>
<p>These are two versions of a drawing I made depicting a content praying rabbit girl. I used a painting of the Virgin Mary for reference. There is no real meaning behind this drawing, I just wanted to capture a certain mood that I can't accurately describe in words.</p>

<p><img src="{{ site.url }}/art/img/2017-original-rabbit-girl.png" class="img-fluid"></p>
<p class="caption"><strong>LorisDown (Original)</strong><br>2017</p>
<p>This drawing was based on the cover of the vaporwave album New Visuals by Bl00dwave, and it became the basis for my first personal character as an adult. Four important themes come to mind when I see this artwork: love, loneliness, and disillusionment in the virtual world.</p>
